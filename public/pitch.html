The product

One product. Three
ways to connect.

Group visits

Two communities pair up for an hour. Bingo, decade trivia, Name That Tune, storytelling. Universal games on the TV in the common room.

Spotlights

One resident is the expert. Lorie on 80s hairstyles. Mr. Henderson on the railroad. Any community on Kindred can tune in.

Pen pals

One-to-one friendships matched by family-submitted profiles. Letters, audio, short videos. The quiet residents finally get connection.

05 / 12 kindred
How it works

As simple as
turning on the TV.

  • The director schedules a session. Five minutes of prep instead of two hours.
  • Residents gather in the common room. They watch the TV — no devices, no logins, no learning curve.
  • The director facilitates. Kindred runs the video, the game, the prompts, the music.
  • Families get a session recap. Photos, quotes, moments. Auto-sent. Tour-closing material.

Why now

The exact moment
this becomes possible.

  • The Surgeon General declared loneliness a public health crisis in 2023. Regulators now measure for it.
  • COVID normalized video calls in nursing homes. Five years ago this idea was impossible to deploy.
  • CMS requires "person-centered" activities for full reimbursement. We help facilities check the box.
  • Activities Director shortage is real. Our software multiplies the one they have rather than requiring a new hire.
